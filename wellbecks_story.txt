WHAT IS COLD-PRESSED JUICE?

Cold-Pressed Juice is made by using a hydraulic press to extract the juice between two stainless steel plates at an incredibly high pressure, extracting the maximum juice possible. This process breaks down the cell walls, unlocking and releasing maximum nutrients.

Unlike centrifugal and masticating juicers, no heat is involved that can cause oxidation. This results in a juice that has more enzymes, vitamins, minerals, and nutrients.
